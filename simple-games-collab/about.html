About Simple Games

Our Story

Simple Games is a collection of nostalgic web games inspired by the early 2000s gaming era. We are Tyler Jung, Yanness Huang, and Mani Kumar, first-year Digital Technologies program students at York University. Currently, we are working at IBM and are dedicated to creating games that bring back the simplicity and fun of those classic games while providing a modern and engaging experience. Join us in reliving the joy of gaming from a simpler time!
